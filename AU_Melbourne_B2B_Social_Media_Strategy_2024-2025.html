1. Unified Strategy Overview

Document Purpose

This document provides a unified monthly content planning and execution guide for E-Sunrise Group Australia's social media marketing efforts across LinkedIn, Facebook, and Instagram. It aligns content with overarching B2B business development objectives for the 2024-2025 period.

Brand Voice

  • Industrial & Supply-Focused: Emphasize manufacturing, supply chain efficiency, and operational excellence.
  • Professional & Technical: Use precise, industry-specific terminology to demonstrate deep expertise.
  • Visually Engaging & Community-Oriented: Use high-quality visuals and foster industry discussions.
  • Solution-Oriented & Partnership-Driven: Focus on solving supply chain challenges and building collaborative relationships.

Target Audience

The primary audience includes professionals within the Australian food industry:

  • Food Manufacturers
  • Distributors & Wholesalers
  • Procurement Professionals
  • Industry Decision-Makers & Influencers

2. Content Pillars & Platform Strategy

Core Content Pillars

  • Industry Expertise & Insights (30-40%): Market analysis, industry trends, technical knowledge. (Lead on LinkedIn/Facebook)
  • Company Credentials & News (25%): Announcements, achievements, team highlights, certifications. (All Platforms)
  • Visual Product & Service Showcase (20-35%): Product information, service capabilities, applications, facility tours. (Lead on Instagram)
  • Community, Partnership & Culture (15-25%): Customer features, partnerships, behind-the-scenes, team spotlights. (Lead on Facebook/Instagram)

Platform-Specific Focus

LILinkedIn: Professional networking, industry expertise, and thought leadership. Focus on long-form content, technical details, and corporate news.

FBFacebook: Community building and engagement. Balance professional content with a more community-oriented approach, including event promotion and discussions.

IGInstagram: Visual brand building and storytelling. Emphasize high-quality visuals, behind-the-scenes content through Stories and Reels, and product showcases.

9. Management & Workflow

Content Workflow

  1. Planning: Identify weekly themes and key messages based on pillars.
  2. Creation: Write post copy and create/source visual assets.
  3. Internal Review: Review by marketing and technical teams for accuracy.
  4. Approval: Final sign-off from leadership.
  5. Scheduling: Schedule approved posts via platform-native tools.
  6. Monitoring & Engagement: Track performance and respond to comments.
  7. Analysis: Review performance to inform future content.

Engagement Protocol

  • Response Time: Respond to comments and messages within 2-4 business hours.
  • Response Quality: Provide helpful, professional, and on-brand responses.
  • Community Building: Proactively engage with content from industry partners and professionals to foster relationships.

Review & Optimization Cadence

Weekly Planning (Mondays):
Review previous week's performance and finalize the current week's content plan.

Monthly Performance Review:
Deep dive into all KPIs, analyze top-performing content, review audience growth, and plan the following month's calendar.

Quarterly Strategy Review:
Comprehensive analysis of the overall strategy, incorporating new market trends and competitive insights to make strategic adjustments.
