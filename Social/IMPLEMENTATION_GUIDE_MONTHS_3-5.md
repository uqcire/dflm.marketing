## üé® Visual Asset Creation Guide

### Brand Consistency

**Color Scheme**:
- Primary: Deep Green (#2C5F2D)
- Secondary: Gold (#D4AF37)
- Accent: Orange (#FF6B35)
- Neutral: Dark Gray (#333333), Light Gray (#F5F5F5)

**Fonts**:
- Headlines: Montserrat Bold
- Body: Open Sans Regular
- Emphasis: Playfair Display

**Logo Usage**:
- Always use high-resolution logo
- Maintain appropriate white space
- Don't stretch or distort logo

### Platform-Specific Specifications

**LinkedIn**:
- Post image: 1200 x 627 px
- Company page cover: 1128 x 191 px
- Logo: 300 x 300 px
- Article featured image: 1200 x 627 px

**Instagram**:
- Feed post: 1080 x 1080 px (square)
- Stories: 1080 x 1920 px (9:16)
- Reels: 1080 x 1920 px (9:16)
- Carousel: 1080 x 1080 px per slide

**Facebook**:
- Post image: 1200 x 630 px
- Cover photo: 820 x 312 px
- Profile picture: 180 x 180 px
- Stories: 1080 x 1920 px

### Content Type Templates

**Infographics**:
- Use Canva or Adobe Illustrator
- Include data visualization
- Keep concise and readable
- Use brand colors and fonts

**Product Photos**:
- High resolution (at least 2000 x 2000 px)
- Good lighting and composition
- Show product details and quality
- Include brand elements

**Video Content**:
- Resolution: 1080p minimum
- Length: LinkedIn 30-90 seconds, Instagram 15-60 seconds
- Include captions (80% of users watch muted)
- First 3 seconds grab attention

## üí¨ Engagement and Community Management

### Response Templates

**Positive Comments**:
```
Thank you for your positive feedback! We're glad to hear about [specific content]. If you'd like to learn more about [related topic], please feel free to send a message or visit our website. Looking forward to working with you!

#E-Sunrise #CustomerSatisfaction
```

**Product Inquiries**:
```
Thank you for your interest in our [product name]! Our technical team would be happy to provide detailed specifications and samples. Please send a DM or email to [email], and we'll respond within 24 hours.

Looking forward to serving you!
```

**Partnership Interest**:
```
Thank you very much for your partnership interest! We're always looking for like-minded partners. Let's schedule a call to discuss specific opportunities. Please send a DM or call [phone] to contact our business development team.

Looking forward to working with you!
```

**Technical Questions**:
```
That's a great technical question! Our expert team can provide detailed answers. To better assist you, please send a DM or email to [technical support email] with your specific needs and application scenarios.

We commit to responding within 24 hours!
```

**Negative Comments** (if any):
```
Thank you for your feedback. We take every customer's experience very seriously. Please send a DM or call [customer service phone] so our team can communicate directly with you, understand the situation, and find a solution.

We commit to resolving your issue as soon as possible.
```

## üö® Common Issues and Solutions

### Issue 1: Low Engagement Rate

**Possible Causes**:
- Poor posting time
- Content not engaging enough
- Poor visual quality
- Irrelevant topics

**Solutions**:
- Test different posting times
- Add interactive elements (questions, polls)
- Improve visual design
- Align closer with audience interests
- Increase video content

### Issue 2: No Leads

**Possible Causes**:
- Unclear call-to-action
- Contact information not obvious
- Content too generic
- Wrong target audience

**Solutions**:
- Strengthen call-to-action
- Simplify contact process
- Provide immediate value (samples, guides)
- Precisely target content
- Proactive outreach

### Issue 3: Negative Comments

**Possible Causes**:
- Product or service issues
- Communication misunderstanding
- Competitor attacks
- Dissatisfied customers

**Solutions**:
- Quick professional response
- Resolve issues privately
- Show willingness to solve
- Follow up to confirm satisfaction
- Learn and improve

### Issue 4: Content Idea Exhaustion

**Possible Causes**:
- Lack of inspiration sources
- Over-reliance on templates
- Don't understand audience needs
- Team fatigue

**Solutions**:
- Follow industry trends
- Collect customer questions
- Team brainstorming
- User-generated content
- Repurpose old content
